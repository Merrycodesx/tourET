01. Bale Mountain tour

Bale Mountains National Park, located in southeastern Ethiopia, is renowned for its diverse ecosystems ranging from lush forests to high-altitude plateaus. It is home to unique wildlife, including the Ethiopian wolf, and offers breathtaking landscapes ideal for trekking and wildlife observation. The park also holds significant cultural value with its rich heritage and indigenous communities.

Erta

01. Erta ale volcanic erruption tour

Erta Ale, located in the Afar Region of northeastern Ethiopia, is one of the world's most active and accessible lava lakes. Known as the "Smoking Mountain" and "Gateway to Hell," this volcano offers a unique and mesmerizing landscape for adventurous travelers. Situated about 600 KM from Addis Ababa, Erta Ale provides a rare opportunity to witness the raw power of Earth's geological forces up close.

Omo

01. Omo valley tour

The Omo Valley, situated in southwestern Ethiopia, is a culturally rich and diverse region home to numerous indigenous tribes with unique traditions and lifestyles. This UNESCO World Heritage site offers breathtaking landscapes and significant archaeological discoveries. Around 700 KM from Addis Ababa, the Omo Valley is a vibrant mosaic of humanity and history.

01. Semen Mountain tour

The Simien Mountains, located in northern Ethiopia, are renowned for their dramatic landscapes featuring jagged peaks, deep valleys, and striking escarpments. This UNESCO World Heritage site is home to unique wildlife such as the Ethiopian wolf and Gelada baboon. It is a prime destination for trekking and offers breathtaking views and rich biodiversity.
